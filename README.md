I research **optimization for deep learning** — how to train and adapt large models faster, cheaper, and more reliably. I am a PhD student at MIPT who combines academic research (**15+ papers**, including top-tier venues) with applied AI in industry, where I lead a practical-AI direction at [Sber](https://www.sberbank.com/), Russia's largest bank.

### 🔬 Research Interests

- **Optimization for ML** — next-generation optimizers ([Muon](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.10777), SignSGD), zero-order / gradient-free methods, warm-up scheduling
- **Efficient LLMs** — parameter-efficient fine-tuning (LoRA / PEFT), pretraining, quantization & inference acceleration
- **Distributed & federated optimization** — decentralized methods, Markovian compression, trusted AI
- **AI Scientist** — autonomous agents for conducting scientific research
